QSeq Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Meerv Inc.

In short: the QSeq desktop apps for macOS and Windows collect no data — no account, no network connections, no analytics. The interactive generator at qseq.app also runs entirely in your browser; the qseq.app marketing website uses Google Analytics to measure aggregate usage.

QSeq is published by Meerv Inc. It comes in two forms — the QSeq desktop apps (macOS and Windows) and the in‑browser tool at qseq.app. This policy explains exactly what each one does with your information.

The QSeq apps (macOS & Windows) — no data collected

  • No personal data. The QSeq apps do not collect any personal information. There is no account or sign‑in.
  • No network, no analytics. The apps make no network connections to Meerv Inc. or anyone else, and contain no analytics, telemetry, advertising identifiers or cookies.
  • Everything stays on your device. The values you enter — GTINs, serial numbers, URLs, logo images and label designs — are processed entirely on your device to generate your codes. They are never transmitted off the device.
  • Your files are yours. Projects and PNG / SVG / PDF exports are saved only to the locations you choose. QSeq does not read files you don’t explicitly open or create.
  • Nothing to share. Because nothing is collected, there is nothing for us to sell, share or retain.

The qseq.app website

  • The generator runs in your browser. The codes you generate on qseq.app are produced locally in your browser and are not sent to our servers.
  • Analytics. The qseq.app website uses Google Analytics (Google LLC) to measure aggregate, anonymized usage — such as page views — so we can improve the site. Google Analytics may set cookies and process technical data such as your IP address, browser type and device type. You can opt out with the Google Analytics opt‑out add‑on, or by blocking cookies/analytics in your browser.
  • Third‑party scripts. To assemble PDF downloads in your browser, the site loads the open‑source jsPDF library from a public CDN (jsDelivr). No data you enter is sent to it.

Children

QSeq is a professional tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Questions about privacy? Email info@meerv.com. Our company‑wide privacy policy for the Meerv website and hosted services is at meerv.com/privacy-policy.
